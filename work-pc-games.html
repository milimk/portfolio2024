My experience in PC and console gaming revolves around designing player-centric experiences for AAA games. I collaborated with cross-functional teams to solve complex gameplay and UI/UX challenges, focusing on enhancing immersion, usability, and replayability in real-time strategy and action-RPG environments.

Key Achievements:
  • Player-Centric Design: Successfully delivered gameplay systems and interfaces that supported diverse player strategies and elevated in-game decision-making.
  • Cross-Team Collaboration: Bridged design, art, and engineering teams to ensure seamless integration of mechanics, resulting in a more polished user experience.
  • Proven Impact: Helped ship award-winning expansions and establish design foundations for a major unannounced title, contributing to positive player feedback and franchise longevity.

These projects strengthened my ability to craft engaging gameplay systems and intuitive UI/UX flows within highly collaborative environments under tight development schedules.



Company of Heroes 2 Expansions


Objective: Enhance strategic depth and mission variety in expansions while preserving the core RTS gameplay that defined the original game.

  • Key Challenges:
    • Designing intuitive UI for new units and abilities without overwhelming players during high-stakes combat.
    • Creating mission content with dynamic objectives to increase replayability and narrative engagement.
  • My Role & Process:
    • Developed and tested UI wireframes for new feature sets (e.g., unit upgrades, campaign selection flow).
    • Worked with narrative and level designers to integrate storytelling into mission briefings and HUD elements.
    • Led user testing sessions, analyzing player behavior to fine-tune tutorials and in-game guidance systems.
  • Impact:
    • These expansions were highly rated by players and media (Metacritic 80+), with users praising UI clarity and the variety of strategic options. My contributions played a key role in extending the title's lifecycle by several years post-launch.

These experiences honed my skills in aligning user behavior with design goals to create lasting impact.

Key Highlights:
  • Design Impact: Helped drive installs, engagement, and monetization by creating intuitive and playful interfaces.
  • Problem Solving: Transformed complex mechanics into approachable, accessible experiences for diverse audiences.
  • Cross-Team Collaboration: Bridged design, development, and product teams to ensure cohesive player journeys.
  • Player-Centered Innovation: Used player feedback and analytics to fine-tune usability and game retention.

My mobile game projects reflect a deep passion for crafting joyful, meaningful experiences. Whether through playful aesthetics or smart design decisions, I aim to make every interaction count.

Jelly Splash


Goal: Create a vibrant puzzle game that maximizes long-term player engagement.

  • Challenges:
    • Striking the right balance between visual charm and gameplay clarity on small screens.
    • Ensuring the UI could support monetization features and new content over time.
  • Process:
    • Designed colorful, cohesive UI elements that complemented the game’s joyful tone.
    • Ran usability studies to optimize navigation and level feedback loops.
    • Worked closely with data analysts to iterate on drop-off points and improve retention.
  • Solution:
    • Contributed to a hit puzzle title that saw over 90 million downloads and strong user reviews on iOS and Android.

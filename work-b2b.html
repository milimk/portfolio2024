BrainTest Clinical App: B2B Collaboration with Universities


My work on the BrainTest Clinical App provided a unique opportunity to collaborate with academic institutions conducting cognitive studies. This experience required balancing the goals of cutting-edge technology with the rigorous demands of academic research.


Goals
  • Create a reliable digital tool for early detection and monitoring of cognitive decline.
  • Support academic research by aligning the app with the specific needs of universities and research institutions.

Challenges
  • Addressing the diverse and complex requirements of different research studies.
  • Ensuring the app met high scientific and clinical standards.
  • Facilitating seamless adoption and integration into university workflows.

Process
  1. Engaging with Academic Institutions:
    • Initiated and maintained partnerships with universities and research teams.
    • Conducted detailed consultations to understand their study protocols and requirements.
  2. Customizing the App:
    • Worked with the development team to tailor features for academic use, such as advanced data tracking and reporting capabilities.
    • Incorporated feedback from researchers to ensure the app’s usability in academic environments.
  3. Onboarding and Support:
    • Collaborated as part of a team to provide training and technical support to university research teams.
    • Created resources and workflows to simplify adoption and maximize efficiency.
  4. Continuous Improvement:
    • Collected user feedback from both researchers and study participants.
    • Contributed to iterative updates to refine the app’s functionality and user experience.

Solution
  • Delivered a flexible, user-friendly tool that seamlessly integrated into academic research workflows.
  • Enhanced the app’s functionality to meet the specific needs of cognitive studies, including customizable data collection and analysis features.
  • Strengthened relationships with universities by providing reliable support and tailored solutions.

Impact
  • Supported groundbreaking research by ensuring the app aligned with academic and clinical standards.
  • Facilitated efficient data collection and analysis, contributing to advancements in cognitive science.
  • Demonstrated the value of collaboration between technology providers and academic institutions, leading to mutual success.

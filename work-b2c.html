BrainTest App


Goal: Design a cognitive health assessment app that’s intuitive and trustworthy for older adults with limited digital experience.

  • Challenges:
    • Accommodating users unfamiliar with smartphones and touch interfaces.
    • Ensuring medical credibility while keeping the UI minimal and non-intimidating.
  • Process:
    • Conducted ethnographic interviews and usability testing with seniors aged 60+ to uncover friction points.
    • Worked with neuroscientists and geriatric experts to validate test logic and tone.
    • Prototyped and iterated using feedback from over 30 user test sessions.
  • Solution:
    • Launched a HIPAA-compliant app with a high completion rate among first-time users, enabling early cognitive screening for 200,000+ individuals across North America.

Mini-Me Kids Brand


Goal: Build a unified digital brand ecosystem for children that balances fun and educational value.

  • Challenges:
    • Maintaining brand coherence across four distinct product lines: Games, Videos, Books, and Learning Academy.
    • Designing content that is both developmentally appropriate and engaging for ages 3–8.
  • Process:
    • Led cross-functional workshops with educators, designers, and stakeholders to define age-specific learning outcomes.
    • Applied UI heuristics and child psychology insights to create intuitive, colorful interfaces that encourage exploration.
    • Developed gamification systems to boost session time and return visits.
  • Solution:
    • Increased user engagement by 40% year-over-year and expanded the brand’s reach into 5 new international markets.

Unlimited Gamez


Goal: Redesign a mobile-first, subscription-based game hub to improve retention and discovery.

  • Challenges:
    • Low session duration and unclear navigation across genres and gameplay types.
    • High churn within the first 7 days of onboarding.
  • Process:
    • Analyzed session heatmaps and funnel data to pinpoint drop-off zones.
    • Introduced a new branding with onboarding flow and content filters to personalize recommendations.
    • Collaborated with developers to improve game loading speeds and mobile responsiveness.
  • Solution:
    • Improved 7-day retention and increased average session length to about 7 minutes through targeted UX changes.

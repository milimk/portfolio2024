BrainTest Customer Management System


Objective: Overhaul the customer management interface to reduce support delays and improve data visibility.

  • Key Challenges:
    • Difficult ticket tracking due to fragmented systems.
    • Limited filtering options for retrieving specific customer cases.
  • Approach:
    • Conducted UX audits and interviews with the support team to uncover friction points.
    • Designed a clean, role-based dashboard with smart filters, quick-access search, and automated ticket tracking.
    • Collaborated with engineers to ensure scalable backend integration with existing tools.
  • Outcome:
    • Reduced average ticket resolution time by 35%.
    • Enhanced customer satisfaction by enabling faster, more personalized support.

Mini-Me Kids Content Management System


Objective: Develop a unified system for managing educational and entertainment content across web and mobile platforms.

  • Key Challenges:
    • Disconnected workflows across teams managing games, videos, books, and learning tools.
    • Quality assurance and brand consistency were difficult to enforce at scale.
  • Approach:
    • Partnered with content providers, engineers, and designers to gather requirements and pain points.
    • Created an easy-to-navigate CMS with drag-and-drop uploading, tagging, preview, and version control.
    • Ran iterative usability tests with stakeholders to fine-tune features and flow.
  • Outcome:
    • Cut content deployment time by 50%.
    • Improved collaboration across remote teams and ensured higher output quality and consistency.

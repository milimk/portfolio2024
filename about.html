Hi, I’m Milim Kim-Sinoy — a UX and Product Designer with with over 10 years of experience creating thoughtful, user-centered experiences across web, mobile, and games.

I’m passionate about designing products that feel intuitive, impactful, and human. Whether refining user journeys, shaping product strategy, or crafting polished visual designs, I love turning complexity into clarity and helping teams build things that truly make a difference.

My work spans B2C apps, games, and internal tools — experiences that have taught me to look at problems from multiple angles and connect design, technology, and collaboration to deliver real results.

I believe great design starts with empathy and curiosity. Both in my work and life, I value diverse perspectives and continuous learning — something that feels natural in a home that blends four nationalities and cultures, reminding me every day of the power of understanding and inclusivity.

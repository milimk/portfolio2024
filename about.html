About Me

Hi, my name is Milim Kim-Sinoy.

With over 10 years of experience across various design roles — Graphic Designer, UI Artist, UX Designer, and Product Manager — I’ve perfected my skills in creating and improving products using a range of creative methods. Whether it’s brainstorming, storyboarding, problem-solving, or conducting user research, I thrive on finding innovative solutions.

Throughout my design career, I’ve always been fascinated by coding. Its logical, structured nature drew me in as a contrast to the creative process. To pursue this passion, I completed a Coding Bootcamp with SheCodes, where I gained proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and frameworks like Bootstrap and React, using tools such as VS Code and GitHub. Writing code that works exactly as envisioned gives me an great sense of accomplishment.

With my combined expertise in design and coding, I bring a unique perspective. I understand both the creative and technical sides of product development, utilizing both the right and left sides of the brain. I’m confident that I can significantly enhance any product by blending these skill sets.

On a personal note, my household represents four nationalities — Canadian, Austrian, Korean, and Filipino — so I deeply value and respect multicultural environments. This appreciation extends to my professional life, where I prioritize diversity and collaboration, cherishing harmony within teams from different backgrounds.
